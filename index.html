The whole idea

One file. In and out of view.

Keep your day in one markdown file, wherever you already write. Drawer reads today's tasks and writes your checkmarks back. That is the entire loop.

Write

Type it anywhere

Add checkboxes under a dated heading in Obsidian, an editor, or iCloud. Or ask an AI to.

Summon

Hit the hotkey

The panel slides out from the top-left edge. Today, carried over, and tomorrow, already sorted.

Focus

Run the timer

Start a focus session on any task. Pomodoro cycles and a stopwatch that logs real hours.

Sync

Check it off

Tap the circle. The checkbox is written straight back into your file. Nothing to sync.

What's inside

Everything optional. Strip it to Minimal, or turn it all on.

Global hotkey

Show and hide from anywhere. No window switching, no clicking around.

⌃⌥Space

Focus timer & Pomodoro

Pause, resume, and a chime at the end. Run focus, short break, long break cycles automatically.

Stopwatch

Log real hours against a task, with an end-of-day summary of where the time went.

Idea board

Jot a thought and swipe over to a board that lives in the same file. Nothing leaves your text.

Notes & teleprompter

A pad with a teleprompter that floats over other apps while you read or present.

Live file sync

Edit in Obsidian, an editor, or iCloud and the panel stays in step. No account, no database.

Focus sounds & check-off chimes

White, pink, brown, green, and ocean noise, plus a pick of satisfying sounds when a task gets ticked. Respects Reduce Motion throughout.

Swipe to act

Swipe a row to delete it or flag it in progress. Add notes as indented lines under a task.

Eight worlds

Calm system glass, or an art-directed world.

Switch in Settings with ⌘,. From a macOS 26 glass plate to a bitmap game window or the Windows XP Luna desktop.

The format

Just markdown checkboxes.

A dated ## heading starts a day. Tasks under it belong to that day. Add (15m) for a focus hint. Indent to write a note.

Easy to write by hand or by an AI. Drop the included skill into your vault and just say "add three tasks for tomorrow."
