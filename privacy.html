Privacy Policy

Last updated July 15, 2026

Drawer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

Your data stays on your Mac

Your todos live in a plain markdown file on your device, in a location you choose. Drawer reads and writes that file and nothing else. The app makes no network requests.

Third parties

Drawer shares no data with third parties, because it has no data to share.
